GWT

https://developers.google.com/web-toolkit/

ocamljs

http://code.google.com/p/ocamljs/

ClojureScript

https://github.com/clojure/clojurescript

http://badassjs.com/post/12648021003/bicavm-a-jvm-implementation-in-javascript
